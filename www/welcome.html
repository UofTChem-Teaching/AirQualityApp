Welcome

As part of Experiment 1, you'll analyze a small subset of ECCC NAPS data, which will be provided to you under the My Date tab. To explore the entirety of O3 and NO2 measurements recorded by the NAPS network across Canada in 2020 see the Explore NAPS tab.

How to navigate the website

This website is broken into six parts: the side panel and the five tabs at the top of the page.

  • The side panel is where you'll find all available NAPS stations for plotting (click on a marker for station details); a dropdown menu to select the station, the date range you'd like to explore, and options to modify the plots (see Explore NAPS tab).
  • Welcome tab, what you're reading right now.
  • My Data tab, where you'll input your student number and get your NAPS data subset for the CHM135 Lab 1 work.
  • Explore NAPS tab which contains the time series and O3 vs. NO2 correlation plots. The side panel options modify these plots.
  • Summary stats tab which displays some simple summary stats of the selected data; this is the same data shown in the Plots tab
  • Notes tab with sources on data.
